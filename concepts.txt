*Ractjs Billion Dollar Mistake: (useEffect infinite loop issue)

1. useEffect er vetor [] na dile seta infinite time loop colte thake

2. useEffect er vetor [state] dile setar [setState] jodi vetorei call kora hoy tahole state update hobar sathe sathe noton kore abar useEffect render houya shuru hoy and etao infinite loop toiri kore.
